About Us


Heyya! Using the MENTALITEASE, we aim to facilitate a systematic approach to diagnosing any mental uneasiness and provide methods to help our users cope with their issues. We provide a step-wise procedure to identify the problem using quiz-like tests and then analyse the intensity of the problem. Based on the intensity, we advice the user about the next steps to be taken inorder to overcome their mental uneasiness. We tie-up with experts and divert our users to such experts based on their condition. If not very intense, we suggest some small-scale activities to ease out stress/anxiety/depression. Our website also has an E-journal where the user can add notes, daily journals and the like.We, aim to be a valuable tool in your life by helping you to be more aware of your mood, behaviour and triggers. But why is it important? Simple!

  • To make better decisions

  • To be more confident

  • To communicate better

  • To build better relationships

  • To be more peaceful
